Bay Area · day trip

Pick a direction.

Three day trips, all under two hours from home.

A breather day after the graduation. Three solid options depending on mood — coast, coast, or trees.

Option A · Coast

Monterey & Carmel

The iconic California coast drive. Aquarium in Monterey, then wander Carmel-by-the-Sea. Long day but unforgettable.

📍 ~1h 40 each way 🌊 Coast 🐠 Aquarium
Option B · Local

Half Moon Bay

Closest of the three. Beach walk, lunch at Sam's Chowder House, quiet harbor. Easy in-and-out half-day.

📍 ~35 min away 🌊 Beach 🦀 Seafood
Option C · Forest

Muir Woods + Sausalito

Drive across the Golden Gate to towering redwoods, then waterfront lunch in Sausalito. Big sky and tall trees.

📍 ~1h 10 away 🌲 Redwoods 🌉 GG Bridge
